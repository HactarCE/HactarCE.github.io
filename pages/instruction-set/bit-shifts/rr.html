 RR op8
Arguments op8 - Any 8-bit register or (HL),(IX+n),(IY+n)
Summary Rotate right
Description Rotates the contents of op8 right by one bit. The leaving (0th) bit is moved into the carry and the carry is moved into the last bit of op8.
Uses Reading bit-wise data, least significant bit first (right-to-left)
Byte Count 2
Clock Cycles
Operand RR RR.L RR.S
(HL) 5 6 6
(IX/Y+n) 7 8 8
r 2 N/A N/A
